About

FarmersLab is a digitized platform for small scale farmers to learn effective application of local pestcides. It is centered on practical learning
for different users with different language backgrounds through animated video guides of steps and precautions to be taken for each pesticide.
FarmersLab also has a number of pesticides recommendations. Recommendations are built based on specific conditions of geographical areas
of farmers. FarmersLab exists to uplift small scale farmers through continuous and sustainable prevention and fighting of pests and diseases.
